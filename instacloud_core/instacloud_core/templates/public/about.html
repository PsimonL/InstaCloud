Welcome to Instacloud!

Instacloud is your go-to platform for sharing adorable pet photos, whether they're of your playful pup or your mischievous cat. With our intuitive interface, you'll find it effortless to navigate and enjoy all the features we offer.

Once you've logged in, you'll be greeted with a delightful carousel of pet pictures from our extensive database.

When you're ready to add your own pet's photo, simply click the "Upload a picture" button displayed on the home page or by selecting the "Upload" option from the navigation bar. You'll then be directed to a page where you can easily upload your image by clicking the "ADD IMAGE" button and selecting the file from your file explorer, or by dragging and dropping the image onto the designated area (as shown in the screenshot below):

After selecting the "Show a wall of" button, which can be found on the home page, or pressing the "Show" option from the navigation bar, you'll be prompted to choose between cats or dogs. Once you've made your selection, you'll be directed to a dedicated gallery page showcasing all the uploaded pictures of the chosen pet.

Additionally, if you'd like to revisit your own uploads, simply navigate to the "Profile" tab. There, you'll find a collection of all the photos you've uploaded so far.
